Localization for AsciiDoctor built-in labels,
prepared by AsciiDoctor developers.
See
https://asciidoctor.org/docs/user-manual/#language-support
https://github.com/asciidoctor/asciidoctor/tree/master/data/locale

Get it by "svn co https://github.com/asciidoctor/asciidoctor/trunk/data/locale"
(you can use SVN to easily download a subdirectory of repo from GitHub).
